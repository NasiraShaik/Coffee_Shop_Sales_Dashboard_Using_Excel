## Project Description
This project focuses on analyzing **coffee shop sales data** to optimize operations, identify high-performing products, and provide actionable insights to increase revenue.

The dashboard uses **interactive Pivot Tables and Charts** in Excel to explore key sales metrics such as:
- Total sales, total transactions, average spend per transaction, average purchase hour
- Sales by **Coffee Type, Day of Week, Time of Day, Month, and Payment Type**
- Trends to support **data-driven business decisions**

## Dashboard Insights
- **Latte** and **Americano with Milk** generate the highest revenue and are most popular.  
- Sales peak around **10‚Äì11 AM** & **4‚Äì5 PM**, indicating key customer traffic hours.  
- Monday & Tuesday see slightly higher sales than other weekdays.  
- Monthly trends highlight **March & October** as top months for revenue.  
- Most customers prefer **card payments**.  

---

## Recommendations
1. **Promote high-revenue coffee types** (Latte, Americano with Milk) with combo offers.  
2. **Optimize staffing** during peak hours (10‚Äì11 AM & 4‚Äì5 PM).  
3. **Introduce loyalty programs** to encourage repeat purchases.  
4. **Track weekend trends** and consider weekend-specific promotions.  
5. **Monitor low-selling coffee types** and adjust inventory accordingly.
